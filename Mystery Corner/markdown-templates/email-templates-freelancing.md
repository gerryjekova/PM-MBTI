# High-Converting Email Templates for Freelancers

## 1. The Referral Request Email

```
Subject: Quick question - know anyone who needs [your service]?

Hi [Name],

I hope this finds you well! I wanted to reach out because I've really enjoyed working with you on [project/service], and I'm currently looking to expand my client roster with similar great-fit businesses.

I'm specifically looking to work with companies that [describe ideal client - industry, size, needs] who need help with [specific service you provide].

Do you know anyone who might be a good fit? If so, I'd be grateful for an introduction. As a thank you, I'd be happy to [offer incentive - discount on future work, free consultation, etc.].

Of course, no pressure if nothing comes to mind right now. I just thought I'd ask since you have such a great network.

Thanks for considering, and I look forward to our continued work together!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## 2. The "Value-First" Prospecting Email

```
Subject: [Specific insight] I noticed about [Their Company]

Hi [Name],

I was reviewing [their website/social media/recent PR] and noticed [specific observation that represents an opportunity or issue].

Based on my experience working with [similar companies/competitors], this often indicates [business implication]. Companies that address this typically see [specific benefit].

Here are three quick suggestions that might help:

1. [Actionable tip they can implement right away]
2. [Another practical suggestion]
3. [Third helpful recommendation]

Feel free to use these ideas whether we work together or not. If you'd like to discuss how I could help implement these strategies (and more) for [their company], I'd be happy to schedule a 20-minute call.

Either way, I hope these insights are helpful!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your profession]
[Website/portfolio link]
```

## 3. The Re-Engagement Email (Past Client)

```
Subject: Catching up + something you might find interesting

Hi [Name],

It's been [timeframe] since we worked together on [previous project], and I wanted to check in to see how things are going. Has [the solution/deliverable] been working well for you?

I recently [completed a similar project/learned a new technique/developed a new service] that made me think of you, specifically because [personalized reason]. You can see an example here: [link if applicable]

I've been working with several clients in [their industry/niche] lately, and I've noticed [trend or insight relevant to their business]. Would you be interested in exploring how this might benefit [their company]?

Either way, it would be great to catch up. How have things been since we last connected?

All the best,
[Your Name]
```
